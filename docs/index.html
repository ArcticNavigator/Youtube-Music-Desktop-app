Tunecat Music

A clean, fast, personal desktop player for YouTube Music.

Tunecat Music is a personal, unofficial desktop client for browsing and playing music from YouTube Music. Sign in with Google to unlock your own playlists and Liked Songs, or use full guest mode with no account at all.

What it does

  • Search and play songs, albums, singles, playlists, and radio mixes.
  • Browse artist and album pages, with synced lyrics where available.
  • Sign in with Google for your own playlists, Liked Songs, and personalized home.
  • Manage your YouTube playlists and download tracks for personal offline use.
  • Full guest mode — search and playback work without signing in.

Privacy

Sign-in is optional, and the app collects as little as possible. Guest mode stores nothing about you. See the full policy for exactly what an optional account record holds and your data rights.
